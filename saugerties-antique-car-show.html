A Look back at the History of the Saugerties Antique Car Show

The Saugerties Antique Car Show has been a staple in the area since the mid 1960s. The show is held annually at the Saugerties Fairgrounds and has been a highlight of the summer season in the picturesque Hudson Valley. The show is a celebration of classic cars, ranging from vintage to modern, and it allows car enthusiasts to come together and share their passion for the automobile. The show also features vendors selling memorabilia, giving the show an old-fashioned feel. It's a great way to take a step back in time and enjoy the nostalgia of classic cars and the stories that go along with them.

How to Get to the Show

The Saugerties Antique Car Show is held at the Saugerties Fairgrounds, which is located at 500 W. Saugerties Road.
