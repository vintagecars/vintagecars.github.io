Are you looking to register an antique car in Delaware? Do you need to know what the process entails and what you need to do? If so, you have come to the right place. This article will provide you with all the information you need to register an antique car in Delaware.

Requirements to Register an Antique Car in Delaware

In Delaware, vehicles that are 25 years old or older can be registered as antique cars. If you have an antique car that you would like to register in Delaware, you must first obtain a valid Delaware registration card. To do this, you must present the Delaware Division of Motor Vehicles with the following documents:

  • A completed Delaware Antique Vehicle Registration Application (Form MV-1)
  • A valid Delaware driver's license or Delaware identification card
  • The vehicle's title
  • Proof of Delaware insurance
  • Proof of Delaware residency

Fees for Registering an Antique Car in Delaware

Once you have all the required documents, you must pay the fees associated with registering an antique car in Delaware. The fees are as follows:

  • $10 registration fee
  • $2.50 title fee
  • $3.50 lien fee (if applicable)
  • $10 plate fee
  • $20.50 plate transfer fee (if applicable)

How to Register an Antique Car in Delaware

Once you have all the required documents and fees, you can register an antique car in Delaware. You can do this in person at the Delaware Division of Motor Vehicles or online at the Delaware DMV website.

Registering an Antique Car In Person

If you choose to register an antique car in Delaware in person, you must visit the nearest Delaware DMV office. When you arrive, you must complete the Delaware Antique Vehicle Registration Application (Form MV-1). You must also present the DMV with all of the documents required for registration. Once all of the documents have been reviewed and approved, you will be given a Delaware registration card.

Registering an Antique Car Online

If you choose to register an antique car in Delaware online, you must visit the Delaware DMV website. Once you have logged in, you will be required to complete the Delaware Antique Vehicle Registration Application (Form MV-1). You must also upload the documents required for registration. Once all of the documents have been reviewed and approved, you will be given a Delaware registration card.

Conclusion

Registering an antique car in Delaware is a relatively simple process. All you need to do is gather the required documents and fees, and then complete the Delaware Antique Vehicle Registration Application (Form MV-1). Once all of the documents have been reviewed and approved, you will be given a Delaware registration card. You can register an antique car in Delaware either in person or online.
