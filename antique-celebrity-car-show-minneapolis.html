Experience The Antique Celebrity Car Show In Minneapolis This Year

For car enthusiasts, the Antique Celebrity Car Show in Minneapolis is a must-attend event. The show offers a unique opportunity to view some of the most iconic vintage cars in the world, all under one roof. This year's show promises to be even bigger and better as it celebrates its 11th anniversary. With over 200 cars on display, the Antique Celebrity Car Show is sure to impress even the most discerning car lover. Here's everything you need to know about the upcoming event.

What to Expect at the Antique Celebrity Car Show

At the Antique Celebrity Car Show, visitors can expect to find a wide variety of classic and vintage cars from different eras. From restored vintage automobiles to brand new luxury cars, the show will have something for everyone. Exhibitors will have the opportunity to showcase their vehicles and visitors can take pictures and get up close and personal with the cars. Plus, there will be special activities, competitions, and even music to keep everyone entertained.

Highlights of the Antique Celebrity Car Show

One of the highlights of the Antique Celebrity Car Show is the competition. A panel of judges will decide the winners in various categories, such as best restoration, best original, and most unique. Winners will receive trophies and cash prizes. In addition, there will be several special awards, such as the People's Choice Award and the Best Interior Award. Visitors will be able to vote for their favorite cars throughout the show.

Notable Guests at the Antique Celebrity Car Show

The Antique Celebrity Car Show will feature some of the most notable names in the car world. There will be special appearances from celebrities, car enthusiasts, and car collectors. Visitors will have the chance to meet these personalities and ask them questions about the cars and their experiences. In addition, there will be a variety of vendors selling car-related merchandise, such as die-cast models, apparel, books, and art.

Entertainment at the Antique Celebrity Car Show

In addition to the cars, the Antique Celebrity Car Show will feature a variety of entertainment. Live bands will be performing throughout the event, as well as DJs and other special entertainment. There will also be a variety of activities for children, including face painting, balloon animals, and interactive games. For adults, there will be a beer garden and food trucks for hungry visitors.

Where and When is the Antique Celebrity Car Show?

The Antique Celebrity Car Show will be held from August 18th to August 21st, 2023 at the Minneapolis Convention Center. The show will be open from 10am to 8pm on Friday and Saturday, and from 10am to 6pm on Sunday. Tickets can be purchased at the door or online in advance.

Conclusion

The Antique Celebrity Car Show is an exciting event for car lovers and enthusiasts alike. With hundreds of cars on display, special guests, and plenty of entertainment, the show is sure to be a memorable experience. Whether you’re an avid collector, a casual fan, or just looking for something fun to do, the Antique Celebrity Car Show is the perfect event for you. So don’t miss out and get your tickets now!
