Sacramento Antique Car Museum – An Unforgettable Experience

The Sacramento Antique Car Museum is one of the most popular attractions in the city of Sacramento, California. Located in the heart of the city, the museum boasts an impressive collection of classic and vintage cars, including some that date back to the early 1900s. Whether you’re an avid car enthusiast or simply looking for a fun family outing, a visit to this museum is sure to be an unforgettable experience.

History of the Museum

The Sacramento Antique Car Museum was established in 1962 by D.C. Smith, a local businessman and car collector. Smith, who had been collecting cars since his youth, wanted to share his passion for antique cars with the world. He opened the museum to the public and it was an immediate success. Over the years, the museum has grown and now houses over 200 cars from all over the world.

The Cars

The cars at the Sacramento Antique Car Museum are truly one of a kind. From classic American makes such as Packard and Ford, to rare European models like Ferrari and Mercedes-Benz, the collection has something for everyone. Visitors can take a self-guided tour of the museum and get up close and personal with the cars. Many of the cars are also available for rent, so you can take a piece of history home with you.

Special Events

The Sacramento Antique Car Museum hosts a variety of special events throughout the year. From car shows to classic car auctions, there’s always something exciting happening at the museum. If you’re looking for a unique and educational experience, be sure to check out the museum’s “Antique Car Boot Camp”, a hands-on workshop where visitors can learn about the history of antique cars and even get to work on a real classic car.

The Cafe

The museum also has its own cafe, where visitors can relax with a cup of coffee or a light snack. The cafe is decorated in classic car memorabilia and features a full menu of hot and cold beverages. The cafe is open throughout the day, so visitors can enjoy a break from exploring the museum.

The Gift Shop

The Sacramento Antique Car Museum also has a gift shop, which sells a variety of souvenirs and memorabilia. From vintage car models to books about classic cars, the gift shop has something for everyone. Be sure to check out the unique collection of antique car-themed books, which offer a fascinating look into the history of classic cars.

Location and Hours

The Sacramento Antique Car Museum is located at 2800 South Street in Sacramento, California. The museum is open seven days a week from 10am-5pm. Admission is $15 for adults and $10 for children. Visit the museum website for more information.

Conclusion

The Sacramento Antique Car Museum is a great place to explore the history of classic cars and get up close and personal with some of the most impressive cars of the past. From rare European makes to classic American models, the museum is a must-see for any car enthusiast or history buff. Be sure to check out the museum’s special events, cafe and gift shop for a truly unforgettable experience.
