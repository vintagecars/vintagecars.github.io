The Polk County N.C Antique Car Show

The Polk County N.C Antique Car Show is one of the largest and most popular events in the area. It features an incredible array of classic cars and hot rods from the 1940s and 1950s. The Cars

At the Polk County N.C Antique Car Show, you will find a wide variety of cars from different eras. The Show Hours
