What Are Antique Plates?

Antique plates are plates that are issued by the state of Virginia for vehicles that are at least 25 years old. These plates are often seen on classic cars, muscle cars, and other vehicles with a historical or sentimental value. They are issued by the Virginia Department of Motor Vehicles (DMV) and are valid for five years before they must be renewed.
