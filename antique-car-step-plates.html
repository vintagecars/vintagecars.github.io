Step plates are a great way to add a touch of style to your classic car. Not only do they add a vintage look, but they also provide a stylish and functional way to access your vehicle. The variety of antique car step plates available in 2023 is vast, so it’s important to choose the right ones for your model. Here’s a guide to the best options to upgrade your classic car’s step plates.

Types of Antique Car Step Plates

When it comes to antique car step plates, there are several types to choose from. The most popular option is the classic step plate, which is designed to mimic the style of an older car. These plates are usually made from durable metal and feature a variety of designs, like a classic checkered pattern or a simple scroll. They can also be painted to match the color of your car. Another option is the modern step plate, which takes inspiration from modern design and features a sleek, contemporary look.

Benefits of Installing Antique Car Step Plates

There are several benefits to installing antique car step plates. The most obvious benefit is that they provide a stylish and functional way to access your vehicle. Step plates also make it easier to get in and out of your car, as they offer a larger platform to stand on. These plates are also a great way to protect your car’s paint job, as they provide a layer of protection against dirt and grime. Finally, step plates can also provide additional traction, making it easier to move around in your vehicle.

How to Choose the Right Step Plate for Your Car

When choosing the right step plate for your car, there are a few things to consider. First, you should consider the style of your car, as the step plates should match the overall look of your vehicle. You should also take into account the size of your car, as the step plates should be large enough to provide adequate footing. Finally, you should consider the material of the step plates, as they should be durable enough to withstand wear and tear.

Where to Find Antique Car Step Plates

There are several places to find antique car step plates. The most obvious place is an auto parts store or a vintage car restoration shop. These stores usually have a wide selection of step plates to choose from, so you can easily find the right ones for your car. You can also search online for step plates, as there are several websites that sell them. Finally, you can also look for vintage car shows, as many of these events feature step plates for sale.

How to Install Antique Car Step Plates

Installing antique car step plates is relatively easy. Most step plates come with mounting hardware and detailed instructions. You should first find the mounting points on your car and then attach the step plates using the provided hardware. You should then make sure the step plates are securely mounted and that they are level. If you are unsure of how to install the step plates, you can always seek help from an experienced auto body shop.

How to Care for Antique Car Step Plates

Once your step plates are installed, you should take care to keep them looking their best. Regularly wiping them down can help prevent dirt and grime from collecting on the surface. You should also be sure to keep them free of rust or corrosion, as this can damage the step plates. Finally, you should also wax the step plates to help protect them from the elements.

The Bottom Line

Antique car step plates are a great way to add a touch of style to your classic car. There are several types of step plates to choose from, and they can provide both style and functionality. When choosing the right step plates for your car, you should consider the style, size, and material of your vehicle. You can find step plates at auto parts stores, vintage car restoration shops, and online. Installing step plates is easy, and you can keep them looking their best with regular cleaning and waxing.
