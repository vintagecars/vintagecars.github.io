History of the Antique Car Club of Memphis

The Antique Car Club of Memphis was founded in 1925 by a group of local car enthusiasts. The club was originally focused on antique vehicles and racing, but it soon expanded to include all types of cars, from classic muscle cars to modern luxury cars. Over the years, the club has grown to include more than 2,000 members.

The club has an active presence in the Memphis area, hosting a variety of events throughout the year. In addition to car shows and cruises, the club also organizes races and rallies. Some of the club’s most popular events include the annual Memphis Motorsports Park race and the Memphis International Raceway Vintage Grand Prix.

The club also hosts the Memphis International Raceway Vintage Grand Prix. This event, which is held in October, features vintage cars from all over the world. Membership in the Antique Car Club of Memphis
