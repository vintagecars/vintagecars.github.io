Auburn, Indiana holds an annual antique car auction that has become one of the most popular events in the state. Each year, collectors from around the country come to the auction to bid on cars that date back as far as the turn of the 20th century. The event is a great opportunity for antique car enthusiasts to purchase rare and valuable cars and restore them to their original glory.

The History of the Auburn Antique Car Auction

The Auburn antique car auction has been a tradition since the early 1970s. It began as a small gathering of car enthusiasts who wanted to show off their prized possessions and find new homes for them. Over the years, the event has grown in popularity and has become one of the most well-attended car auctions in the Midwest. The event is now held every year in late June and draws hundreds of bidders from all over the world.

The Selection of Cars

The Auburn antique car auction is known for its vast selection of cars. Most of the cars that are up for auction are from the early 1900s up until the 1950s. These cars are often in original condition and have been kept in impeccable condition. Some of the cars that are available for purchase are rare and valuable, making them a great investment for any car collector.

The Auction Process

The auction process at the Auburn antique car auction is fairly straightforward. Each car is given an estimate price and then bidders must compete to win the car. The bidding starts low and then increases as the auction progresses. The highest bidder at the end of the auction is declared the winner. The auction also offers a number of other services such as financing, appraisals, and insurance.

The Benefits of Attending the Auction

Attending the Auburn antique car auction provides a number of benefits for car enthusiasts. It offers a chance to find rare and valuable cars at a fraction of the cost of buying them from a dealer. It also provides an opportunity to meet other car enthusiasts and make connections with potential buyers. The auction also serves as a great opportunity to learn more about the history of antique cars and the process of restoring them.

The Cost of Attending the Auction

The cost of attending the Auburn antique car auction varies depending on the type of car you are interested in buying. Most of the cars are sold for around $1500 to $5000, although some of the more rare and valuable cars can sell for much more. It is important to remember that the price of a car can also go up significantly depending on the condition and age of the car.

Tips for Buying at the Auction

When attending the Auburn antique car auction, it is important to remember a few tips to ensure that you get the best deal possible. First, it is important to inspect the car thoroughly before bidding and make sure that it is in good condition. Additionally, it is important to research the car before bidding to make sure that you fully understand its value. Finally, it is important to be prepared to pay the price that you bid on the car, as it is not always possible to haggle or negotiate on the price.

Conclusion

The Auburn antique car auction is one of the most popular events in the Midwest and attracts car enthusiasts from all over the world. It is a great opportunity to purchase rare and valuable cars at a fraction of the cost and to meet other car enthusiasts. It is important to remember to inspect the car before bidding and to understand its value before making an offer. With the right preparation, you can find the perfect car for your collection at the Auburn antique car auction.
