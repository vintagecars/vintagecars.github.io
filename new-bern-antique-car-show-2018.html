The annual New Bern Antique Car Show is an event that is always eagerly anticipated by classic car enthusiasts. This year was no exception, with the show taking place on the weekend of August 17th and 18th. Hundreds of classic cars, trucks, and motorcycles filled the event grounds, making it a truly unique experience for all the attendees. The show was held at the New Bern Convention Center, conveniently located near the city's downtown area.

A Show Like No Other

The New Bern Antique Car Show is like no other car show in the country. It is the only show that focuses exclusively on classic cars and trucks from the 1950s and 1960s. The vehicles on display ranged from pristine condition vintage models to heavily modified hot rods and rat rods. There were plenty of vendors selling classic car parts and accessories, as well as food and drinks to keep everyone fed and happy.

Highlights of the Show

One of the highlights of the show was the parade of cars, which took place on the second day. The parade was an impressive sight, with dozens of classic cars driving through the streets of New Bern. Many of the cars were decorated with streamers, balloons, and other festive decorations. It was an incredible sight to see and one that will be remembered for years to come.

Awards and Special Events

The show also featured an awards ceremony for the best classic cars in various categories. The awards were given out in categories such as best original, best modified, and best classic car of the show. Additionally, there were a number of special events such as a burn-out competition and a drag racing exhibition. The burn-out competition was especially popular with the crowd, as the competitors put on a spectacular show.

The People Who Make the Show Possible

The success of the show is largely due to the hard work of the volunteers and staff who make it possible. The volunteers come from all walks of life, with many of them being classic car enthusiasts themselves. The staff is responsible for making sure the show runs smoothly and that everyone has a great time. Without their hard work and dedication, the New Bern Antique Car Show would not be the success that it is.

A Unique Experience

The New Bern Antique Car Show is a unique event that brings together classic car enthusiasts from all over the country. It is an event that celebrates the history of classic cars and allows people to admire the hard work and dedication that went into restoring them. For anyone who loves classic cars, the New Bern Antique Car Show is an event that should not be missed.

Conclusion

The New Bern Antique Car Show 2018 was a great success and an experience that will be remembered for years to come. The show was filled with classic cars, trucks, and motorcycles from the 1950s and 1960s, as well as vendors and special events like the parade and the burn-out competition.
