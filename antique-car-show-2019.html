This year, 2019, the Antique Car Show is back in town! The show will be held on the 20th of October and promises to be bigger and better than ever. This year’s show will feature an impressive display of classic cars from all over the world, including some of the most iconic makes and models. Whether you’re a lover of classic cars or simply interested in the nostalgia of the cars, this show is sure to make an impression.

What to Expect

At this year’s show, you can expect to see a wide variety of classic cars from a number of different eras. From early Ford Model Ts to classic muscle cars of the '60s, the show will have something for everyone. In addition to the cars, there will also be a selection of memorabilia, books, and other items related to classic cars on display. There will also be a number of vendors offering parts and services for classic cars.

Meet the Owners

One of the highlights of the show is the opportunity to meet the owners of these classic cars. The owners will be on hand to answer any questions you may have, as well as to share stories about their cars. It’s a great chance to learn more about the history of classic cars and to gain insight into the passion and dedication of the owners. Even if you’re not a car enthusiast, it’s a great opportunity to connect with people who share a common interest.

The Judges

This year’s show will feature a panel of expert judges to review the cars on display. The judges will be evaluating the cars on a number of criteria including originality, condition, and overall presentation. There will be awards given out in a variety of categories, so it’s sure to be a highly competitive event.

Other Events

In addition to the car show itself, there will be a number of other events taking place during the show. There will be an auction where you can bid on classic cars and memorabilia. There will also be a car cruise around the city, giving you a chance to see some of the cars in action. There will be live music, food vendors, and more, making the show a great opportunity for a full day of entertainment.

Getting There

The show will be held at the city’s convention center, located just a short drive from downtown. There will be plenty of parking available, and the area is easily accessible by public transportation. If you’re looking for a unique way to get to the show, you can take advantage of the vintage car rides that will be available.

Conclusion

The Antique Car Show 2019 promises to be an exciting and unforgettable event. If you’re a lover of classic cars or simply looking to experience the nostalgia of a bygone era, this show is sure to be a great experience. Be sure to mark your calendars for the 20th of October and don’t miss out on this once-in-a-lifetime opportunity.
