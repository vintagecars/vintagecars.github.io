Ocean City Nj Antique Car Show 2015 – An Event To Remember

The Ocean City NJ Antique Car Show 2015 was something that would be forever etched in the memories of all those who attended. Held on the 5th of June 2015, the show was a spectacular display of classic cars from all over the world. From vintage Fords to classic Chevrolets, the show had something for everyone.

The event was held at the Ocean City Boardwalk, giving attendees a chance to take in the beautiful scenery while enjoying the show. The show organizers had worked hard to make sure that the event was a success, and they certainly achieved their goal. The show was packed with cars from all around the world, giving attendees a chance to admire the craftsmanship and engineering that went into making these cars.

There was an expansive array of activities available to participants, ranging from a scavenger hunt, to a live band playing music and a car show. The scavenger hunt was a great way for participants to explore the boardwalk and get to know the area better. The live music was a great way to keep the crowd entertained and the car show was a great opportunity for car enthusiasts to show off their prized possessions.

The Special Guests

The show also had some special guests that added to the atmosphere of the event. The first was the legendary film director, actor and producer, Steven Spielberg. Spielberg was there to talk about the importance of preserving the classic car culture and his latest film, Bridge of Spies. He was also there to give away some awards to the most impressive cars in the show.

The second special guest was the famous artist and car enthusiast, Jay Leno. Leno was there to talk about the importance of preserving the classic car culture and his own personal experiences with the cars at the show. He also spoke about the importance of preserving the original parts of the cars, in order to ensure that they remain in their best condition.

The Awards

At the end of the day, awards were given to the best cars of the show. The cars that made the cut were a 1933 Buick Roadmaster, a 1957 Chevrolet Bel Air, a 1961 Chevrolet Impala, a 1964 Ford Thunderbird, a 1966 Pontiac GTO, a 1967 Chevrolet Corvette, and a 1968 Pontiac Firebird. Each of these cars had something special that made them stand out from the rest. The award-winning cars were all in excellent condition and had been kept in pristine condition.

The Ocean City NJ Antique Car Show 2015 was definitely an event to remember. The show was a great opportunity for car enthusiasts to show off their prized possessions, while enjoying the beautiful scenery of the boardwalk. The special guests who attended the show added to the atmosphere and the awards given out were a testament to the amazing vehicles that were on display. For those who are interested in classic cars, the Ocean City NJ Antique Car Show 2015 was certainly an event to remember.
