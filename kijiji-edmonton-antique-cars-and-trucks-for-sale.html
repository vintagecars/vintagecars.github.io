Searching for Antique Cars and Trucks on Kijiji Edmonton

When searching for an antique car or truck on Kijiji Edmonton, you will want to start by narrowing down your search to a specific make and model. This can be done by checking the “Make & Model” box and then selecting the specific make and model that you are interested in. You can then add additional search criteria such as year, price range, and condition to further narrow down your results.

Once you have narrowed your search, you will be presented with a list of antique cars and trucks that meet your criteria. You can then review the individual listings to determine which vehicle is the best fit for you. Each listing will provide you with information about the vehicle, including the year, make, model, mileage, and condition.

Contacting the Seller of an Antique Car or Truck

Once you have found an antique car or truck that you are interested in, you will want to contact the seller to find out more information. Kijiji Edmonton makes it easy to contact the seller by providing you with their contact information. You can also use the “Contact Seller” button to send a message directly to the seller.

When contacting the seller, it is important to be polite and professional. Ask any questions that you may have about the vehicle, and make sure to request additional pictures if needed. The seller should be willing to answer your questions and provide you with additional information about the vehicle.

Buying an Antique Car or Truck from Kijiji Edmonton

Once you have decided to purchase an antique car or truck from Kijiji Edmonton, you will need to make arrangements to pay for the vehicle. Kijiji offers a variety of payment options, including cash, cheque, credit card, PayPal, and bank transfer. Be sure to read the seller’s terms and conditions before agreeing to purchase the vehicle.

Once the payment has been made, you will need to arrange for delivery or pick-up of the vehicle. If you are unable to pick up the vehicle yourself, you can arrange for a shipping service to pick up the vehicle and deliver it to your home or office. Be sure to read the shipping terms and conditions carefully before agreeing to the service.

Financing Your Purchase of an Antique Car or Truck
