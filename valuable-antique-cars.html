What is an Antique Car?

An antique car is a vehicle that is more than 25 years old. The term antique car is typically used to refer to vehicles that are considered to be "classics" or collectibles. Although the exact definition of an antique car varies from one collector to another, the most common criteria for classifying a car as an antique are that it must be at least 25 years old and must be considered to be of historical or sentimental interest. Antique cars can range from classic American muscle cars from the 1950s to vintage European sports cars from the 1930s.
