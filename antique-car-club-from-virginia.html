The Antique Car Club from Virginia (ACCV) is an organization dedicated to the appreciation and preservation of antique cars. Founded in 2023, the ACCV has grown to become one of the largest antique car clubs in the United States, with over 500 members. The club is open to anyone with an interest in antique automobiles, regardless of age, background, or skill level.

The ACCV holds two large events every year, one in the spring and one in the fall. The spring event is a car show that attracts hundreds of antique cars from all over the country. The fall event is a car rally that takes place in the Blue Ridge Mountains of Virginia. Both events feature a variety of activities and competitions, and offer members a chance to connect with other antique car enthusiasts.

The club also offers a variety of educational and social activities throughout the year. These include monthly meetings, car shows, lectures, and field trips. The club also maintains a library of books and videos on antique cars, and provides technical support to members.

Membership Benefits

Members of the ACCV enjoy a variety of benefits, including discounts on car parts and services, access to special events, and the chance to meet and network with other antique car enthusiasts. The club also offers a variety of discounts on car parts and services from some of the nation’s leading automotive retailers.

Members of the club also receive a quarterly newsletter that contains information about upcoming events, car shows, and other news. The newsletter also includes articles about antique cars and the hobby in general. Members also receive a membership card that allows them to receive discounts at car shows and other events.

The ACCV’s Mission

The mission of the ACCV is to promote the appreciation, preservation, and restoration of antique cars. The club strives to provide a supportive and educational environment for members, and to share their enthusiasm for antique cars with others. The goal of the club is to help members develop the skills necessary to maintain and restore antique cars, and to promote the hobby in general.

The ACCV also works to preserve the history of antique cars and to educate the public about the importance of preserving these classic automobiles. The club works with local car clubs, museums, and other organizations to promote antique cars and the hobby.

Joining the ACCV

Anyone with an interest in antique cars is eligible to join the ACCV. Membership is open to all ages, and there are no restrictions on the type of car you own or the level of knowledge you have. Membership fees are affordable, and new members receive a welcome package that includes a membership card, a newsletter subscription, and access to the club’s library of books and videos.

If you’re interested in joining the Antique Car Club from Virginia, visit the club’s website for more information. You can also contact the club directly to ask any questions you may have. The ACCV welcomes all enthusiasts of antique cars and is dedicated to promoting the preservation of these classic automobiles.
