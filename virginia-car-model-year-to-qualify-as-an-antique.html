Maintenance Requirements for Virginia Antique Vehicles

In Virginia, an antique vehicle must be maintained in its original condition. Virginia's Antique Vehicle Registration Requirements

In order to register an antique vehicle in Virginia, the vehicle must be inspected by a local law enforcement officer. Insurance Requirements for Virginia Antique Vehicles

Virginia's Antique Vehicle License Plate Requirements
