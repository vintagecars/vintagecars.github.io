Antique cars are a hot commodity in India, especially amongst classic car collectors. From the 1920s Austin 7 to the 1950s Hindustan Ambassador, the Indian market is full of classic cars that can be restored to their original glory. But where do you start when looking for an antique car in India? We have compiled a comprehensive guide to help you out.

Types of Antique Cars in India

When it comes to antique cars in India, there is a wide variety to choose from. Some of the most popular classic cars in the country include the Austin 7, the Morris 8, the Hindustan Ambassador, and the Standard 10. All of these cars were manufactured in India during the early to mid 20th century.

The Austin 7 was produced by the Austin Motor Company between 1922 and 1939. This car was the first mass-produced vehicle in India, and it was the most popular car of its time. The Morris 8 was another popular car of its era, manufactured between 1934 and 1948. It was known for its reliability, as it could easily run for long distances without breaking down.

The Hindustan Ambassador was the most popular car in India during the 1950s and 1960s. It was a four-door family car that was known for its durability and comfort. The Standard 10 was a four-door sedan produced in India between 1950 and 1962. It was known for its smooth handling and power.

Finding an Antique Car in India

If you are looking for an antique car in India, the best place to start is online. There are a variety of online platforms that sell vintage cars, such as eBay India and OLX. You can also find vintage cars for sale in newspapers and magazines, as well as at local car shows.

When looking for an antique car in India, it is important to do your research. Make sure you thoroughly inspect the car before you make a purchase. Check the engine, transmission, brakes, and other mechanical components to make sure they are in good condition. You should also look for rust and other signs of wear and tear.

Once you have found the right car, you should consider getting it professionally restored. Many classic car restoration companies in India specialize in restoring antique cars to their original condition. This is a great way to ensure that your car is in the best condition possible.

Cost of Restoring an Antique Car in India

The cost of restoring an antique car in India depends on the type of car and the amount of work needed to restore it. Generally, the cost of restoring a classic car in India can range from a few thousand rupees to several lakhs. This cost can vary depending on the type of car and the amount of restoration work required.

If you don’t have the time or the money to restore an antique car in India, you can always buy a pre-restored car. Many classic car restoration companies offer pre-restored cars that are ready to be driven. This is a great option if you want to get your hands on an antique car without the hassle of doing the restoration work yourself.

Conclusion

Antique cars are a great way to experience the nostalgia of the past. Whether you want to buy a pre-restored car or restore a car yourself, there are plenty of options available in India. With a little bit of research, you can find the perfect antique car for your collection.
