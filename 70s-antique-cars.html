Ford Mustang

The Ford Mustang needs no introduction. This iconic car first appeared in the 60s, but it was in the 70s when it truly took off. The 70s saw the introduction of the Mach 1 and the Boss 302, two of the most iconic Mustangs ever made. The Mustang was popular because it was affordable and had a wide variety of engines available. From the 302 V8 to the 351 Cleveland, the Mustang had an engine option to please everyone. This car was perfect for those who wanted to look cool and have some fun.

Chevrolet Chevelle

The Chevrolet Chevelle was an instant hit when it was introduced in the 70s. This car was a perfect combination of performance and style. It had a wide variety of options available, from the standard straight-six engine to the big block 454 V8. The Chevelle was a great choice for those who wanted something that could handle the highway and look good while doing it. The Chevelle was so popular in the 70s that it was the basis for many of the popular muscle cars of the time.
